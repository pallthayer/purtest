Browsing People Profiles and Pools

How do determine I if someone I know is a member?

You can use the alphabetical index on the Browse Profiles page.

How do I find people who live near me?

Use the map on the Browse Profiles map. You can zoom and pan the map to center it on the geographic area where you live. The markers on the map repesent zipcodes where Purpool members live. When you click on a marker the people at your workplace who have joined Purpool and who live in that zipcode are listed to the left of the map. Click on a name to see their profile.

How do I find out if there are existing pools near me?

Use the map on the Browse Pools map. You can zoom and pan the map to center it on the geographic area where you live. The markers on the map repesent zipcodes where there are active pools. When you click on a marker the pools in that zipcode are listed to the left of the map. Click on a pool name to see the pool profile.
